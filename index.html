Frequently asked questions

What model are your AI agents built on?

We use a multitude of open-source and commercial models. The models we use are interchangeable and fine-tuned depending on business requirements. Get in contact to learn about the different models we use.

How do you offer deployment?

Based on preference, we can host on our cloud servers or we can set up on your own cloud.

Where does the data come from?

To provide you with the best data-decisioning experience, we integrate into your internal data sources and relevant APIs only. We refrain from using any external data to eliminate errors caused by data contamination.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry standard encryption techniques to store and transmit your data. We can host our models and software in your own cloud which means data never leaves your systems.

How long does it take to implement?

CMND integration takes between 1 month up to 3 months for most businesses. We already have a library of more than 150 business platforms and databases that we can integrate. Our integration specialists will spend some time with stakeholders from your business to understand your needs and tailor CMND configuration accordingly.
